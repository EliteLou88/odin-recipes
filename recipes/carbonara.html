Carbonara

Beautiful, authentic and aromatic

This Italian dish is made with traditional ingredients and made in a way which is true to it's origin. There is no supermarket made sauce in use here!

Ingredients

  • 75g spaghetti
  • 1tbs olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 large egg
  • 30g finely grated parmesan
  • 90g pancetta

Steps

  1. Bring large saucepan of water to the boil and add pasta for 8 mins.
  2. Add olive oil to frying pan and bring to medium heat
  3. Add pancetta and garlic to the pan, season with black pepper and fry for 8 mins.
  4. To make sauce add 1 egg to a stirrable container, whisk with black pepper and 15g parmesan
  5. Remove garlic from the pan and add pasta and 3 tbs of pasta water.
  6. Toss pasta and pancetta together for 2 mins
  7. Remove pan from heat for 1 min then add sauce. Toss and cover the pasta
  8. Serve on a plate, and top the dish with remaining parmesan and black pepper
