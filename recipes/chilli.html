Chilli Con Carne

Beautiful, authentic and aromatic

This classic uses common ingredients to arrive at a staple dish for the majority of families.

Ingredients

  • 500g Beef Mince
  • 1 Onion
  • 400g chopped tomatoes
  • 400g kidney beans
  • 200g water
  • 2 carrots
  • 1 bell pepper
  • 2tbs chilli powder
  • 1tbs cumin
  • 1tbs smoked paprika
  • 1 beef stock cube
  • 2tbs olive oil

Steps

  1. Place olive oil in a wok on medium heat, add 500g mince and fry off until brown.
  2. Add chopped bell pepper and onions along with salt and pepper to taste.
  3. When onions and peppers have softened (5 mins) add chopped tomatoes, water and bring to a simmer.
  4. Add beef stock cube.
  5. Add chilli powder, cumin, spoked paprika.
  6. Add finely chopped carrots.
  7. Simmer for 40mins, season to taste and serve.
