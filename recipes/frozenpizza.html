Frozen Pizza

Beautiful, authentic and aromatic

A frozen pizza is a thing of beauty, no time to prepare, just stick it in the oven and you are on your way. Brilliant.

Ingredients

  • Pizza bought from any supermarket freezer section

Steps

  1. Take the pizza out of the packaging
  2. Place in the oven for 12 mins at 200c/180c fan
  3. Remove from oven and slice quarter ways to serve
